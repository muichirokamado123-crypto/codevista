# Brand Guidelines — CodeVista

## Brand Identity

- **Name:** CodeVista
- **Tagline:** "Vision Through Code"
- **Location:** Bahria Town Phase 7, Rawalpindi, Pakistan

## Color Palette

| Token      | Hex       | Usage                  |
|------------|-----------|------------------------|
| Primary    | #2563eb   | Buttons, links, CTAs   |
| Accent     | #0ea5e9   | Highlights, hovers     |
| Background | #ffffff   | Page background        |
| Foreground | #1e293b   | Body text              |
| Muted      | #f1f5f9   | Card backgrounds       |
| Border     | #e2e8f0   | Borders, dividers      |

### Extended Palette
- Primary Dark: #1d4ed8 (hover states)
- Primary Light: #dbeafe (light backgrounds)
- Success: #10b981
- Error: #ef4444
- Warning: #f59e0b

## Typography

- **Font Family:** Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Headings:** Inter Bold (700) / Semibold (600)
- **Body:** Inter Regular (400)
- **Small:** Inter Medium (500)

### Scale
- H1: 3rem (48px) — Hero headings
- H2: 2.25rem (36px) — Section headings
- H3: 1.5rem (24px) — Card titles
- Body: 1rem (16px) — Paragraphs
- Small: 0.875rem (14px) — Captions

## Logo

- **Icon:** Stylized angle brackets forming a "V" (< V >)
- **Wordmark:** "CodeVista" in Inter Bold
- **Color:** Primary Blue #2563eb on white, White on dark backgrounds
- **Files:** logo.svg, logo-icon.svg, favicon.svg

## Voice & Tone

- Professional yet approachable
- Technical but accessible
- Confident, not boastful
- Focus on client outcomes and solutions
